About MUN

Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad is proud to present its second edition of MNNIT MUN’18 under the aegis of the institute’s annual cultural festival “Culrav 2018 – A Tryst with the Darkness”. The vision and mission of the conference is quite clear by its motto itself which says “Veni Vidi Vici”. Taking this obligation in mind, we are yet again back to offer an ultimate show of a three-day debate and deliberation comprising of three committees which will offer the delegates a true insight of an MUN conference. We’d be looking forward for an enthusiastic response as we open the delegate (individual) and delegation application form. The previous edition of MNNIT MUN saw immense participation from numerous schools and colleges with an elite Executive Board and diligent Organising Committee members. We hope to get an optimum response this year as well and the conference will be, for sure, worth remembering.

LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

Dear Delegates,

It is with great pleasure I, Secretary General MNNIT MUN 2018, on behalf of the organising committee welcome you to the second edition of MNNIT MUN under the aegis of Culrav 2018 – A tryst with the darkness, to be held from Thursday,22nd of March to Saturday, 24th of March, 2018.

Though MNNIT MUN is one of the upcoming conferences of the country, we here at MNNIT Allahabad are committed to providing the most enriching delegate experience possible. With 3 different councils and an expected participation of over 150 delegates from prestigious institutes all over the country, the second edition shall witness the best diplomacy and best debate, to establish itself as one of the most efficient MUNs all across the country.

Whether you’re a veteran or a first-time delegate, I am sure the conference will provide you the right platform to meaningfully engage with contemporary world issues, polish public speaking and communication skills, and become more confident and articulate global citizens.

Please browse through our website and our facebook page to see all of the committees that will be simulated at MNNIT MUN 2018, and if you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,

Riyaz Panjwani
Secretary General
MNNIT MUN 2018
